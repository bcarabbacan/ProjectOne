Ranking Pokémon Games I've Played Recently

  1. White
    2.

    I absolutely loved the soundtrack and the story. The first Pokémon game I played starting my marathon of Pokémon games near the end of 2024.

  2. Legends: Arceus
    3.

    This provided a different way of playing Pokémon and the new way of catching Pokémon was addicting. I also enjoyed the increased availability of shiny Pokémon and the difficulty.

  3. Black 2
    4.

    This was very good. It added quality of life updates from White/Black and it was a direct sequel. I just prefered the story and Team Plasma soundtrack on the first one more.

  4. Sword
    5.

    My most recent, I enjoyed playing through the story and I liked the increased personalization of your character. Just the options were dissapointing for me, the male protagonist specifically. The graphics were also nice.

  5. Shining Pearl
    6.

    My first Nintendo Switch Pokémon game. My friend's brother let me borrow his copy! I also got my first full odds shiny here!
